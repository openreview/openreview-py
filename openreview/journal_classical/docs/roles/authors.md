# Authors

## Your Work

- Submit new JMLR manuscripts and resubmissions.
- Keep the Author List, affiliations, OpenReview IDs or emails, and conflict declarations accurate.
- Respond to required author tasks, especially camera-ready revision requests after acceptance.
- Read decisions, released reviews, and official paper communication when they become available.

## Where To Go

Use the venue landing page's `New Submission` entry point to submit a new manuscript. You do not need pre-existing `JMLR/Authors` membership to submit; the submitted Author List determines who becomes an author for the paper.

Use the previous paper page's `Resubmission` author action to submit a revised manuscript after an eligible decision, such as `Accept after minor revisions` or `Reject with encouragement to resubmit`. That action opens the JMLR submission editor with the previous paper number prefilled, and carries forward the previous Author List and Open Source Software selection.

Use the Author Console to find papers where you are listed as an author and to complete author tasks. Paper links open the standard OpenReview paper page.

## Main Actions

| Action | When to use it |
|---|---|
| New Submission | Submit a new manuscript from the venue submission entry point. |
| Resubmission | Submit a revised manuscript from the previous paper page after an eligible decision. |
| Author List | Enter every author by OpenReview profile ID, in paper order, for example `~First_Author1, ~Second_Author1`. Every author must have a profile ID, and the submitting author's profile ID must be included. |
| Volunteer as JMLR Reviewer | Record willingness to join the JMLR reviewer pool. This is separate from submission and does not assign you to any paper. If added to the pool, you can adjust your maximum active review load later from the Reviewer Console. |
| Conflict Of Interests | List OpenReview profile IDs for conflicted people. Mention other conflicts in the cover letter; they may not be processed as structured OpenReview conflicts. |
| Contact AE | Send restricted author-to-editor paper communication when available. |
| Camera-ready revision | Upload final material after acceptance when requested. |

## Notes

Submission and resubmission field rules are owned by the
[lifecycle](../workflow/lifecycle.md) and visible submission form.

JMLR review is single blind. Editors and reviewers can see author identities, but authors should not see reviewer or Action Editor identities.

For a resubmission, use the previous paper page action. The Author List and Open Source Software selection carry forward; only author ordering changes are allowed.

If a button or task is missing, first check that you are in the Author Console role context and on the correct paper page.

Reviewer volunteering is described in
[Role Recruitment And Volunteer Responses](../workflow/role-recruitment.md).
