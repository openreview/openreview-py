# Camera-Ready Revision

Accepted papers move through camera-ready revision and verification before
publication handling.

| Action | Role | Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Submit camera-ready revision | Author | Uploads final paper material and publication metadata. |
| Verify camera-ready revision | Action Editor or EIC | Approves final material or asks authors for changes. |

Camera-ready controls are stage-specific. They should not appear for ordinary
submitted, under-review, rejected, or already published papers.

## Revision Fields

The author revision form should collect the final paper file, publication
metadata, author ordering, final affiliations, optional supplementary material
when allowed, and any required style or production metadata. The original
submission metadata remains the comparison baseline; the direct camera-ready
path is not a general author-replacement path.

Final author-list validation should preserve the accepted paper's authors while
allowing only the ordering and metadata updates that the camera-ready workflow
explicitly permits.

## Verification Outcomes

The verification action should give the Action Editor or Editor-in-Chief enough
information to approve the camera-ready material or request another revision.
Approval moves the paper toward publication handling. A revision request returns
the paper to the author-facing camera-ready task without changing earlier review
or decision records.
