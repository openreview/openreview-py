# Author Console

The Author Console summarizes submissions and author tasks.

| Area | Shows | Expected Action |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Your submissions | Submitted papers and their current author-facing status. | Open the paper in author role context. |
| Pending tasks | Author actions such as revision, resubmission, or camera-ready revision when available. | Complete the visible paper-specific form. |
| Contact AE | Paper-specific communication when allowed. | Send author clarification to the handling AE. |

Must hide: reviewer identities, unreleased reviews, EIC/AE/Production Editor
operational controls, and reviewer-only tasks.

Pending tasks should represent required paper-specific author actions. Optional
comments or informational messages should remain available from the paper page
when allowed, but should not create pending-task rows by themselves.
